Information Architecture

Hypothesis: Reorganizing for the Developer Lifecycle

Currently, the "Dashboard vs SDK" split can create cognitive load. I suspect we could reduce friction by aligning the navigation to the developer's journey:

01. Foundation (Day 1)

"Get me integrated." Unified quickstarts that combine Dashboard and SDK setup into a single successful purchase flow.

02. Conversion (Day 7)

"Get me paid." Grouping Paywalls, Offerings, and Web Billing into a cohesive "Store Building" ecosystem.

03. Optimization (Day 30)

"Get me more." Moving high-value growth tools like Experiments and Targeting from sub-menus to the forefront.

04. Operations (Ongoing)

"Help me scale." Creating a dedicated home for troubleshooting, customer support tools, and data exports.
